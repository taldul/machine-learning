2 Data ¶

The dataset consists of thre Titanic's passengers information. It can be found here.

Variables:

  • PassengerId - the id of the passenger (unique number).
  • Survived - if the person survived (0 = No, 1 = Yes).
  • Pclass - ticket class (1 = 1st class, 2 = 2nd class, 3 = 3rd class).
  • Name - passenger name.
  • Sex - the sex of the passenger (male, female).
  • SibSp - the number of siblings or spouses aboard the Titanic.
  • Parch - the number of parents or children abroad the Titanic.
  • Ticket - ticket number.
  • Fare - passenger fare.
  • Cabin - passenger cabin number.
  • Embarked - port of embarkation (C = Cherbourg, Q = Queenstown, S = Southampton).
